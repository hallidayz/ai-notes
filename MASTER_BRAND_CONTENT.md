# Master Brand Content

## Color Palette

### Primary Brand Colors (Light Mode)

#### Authority Navy
- **Hex Code:** `#02295b`
- **Usage:** Primary brand color, headers, trustworthy foundations
- **Purpose:** Establishes authority and trustworthiness in the interface

#### Achievement Gold
- **Hex Code:** `#fda700`
- **Usage:** Success indicators, CTAs (Call-to-Action buttons), achievement highlights
- **Purpose:** Draws attention to important actions and celebrates success

#### Strategic Forest
- **Hex Code:** `#2c5f41`
- **Usage:** Growth-minded creative elements and strategic innovation
- **Purpose:** Represents growth, creativity, and strategic thinking

#### Pure Foundation
- **Hex Code:** `#f6f7f9`
- **Usage:** Clean backgrounds, clarity
- **Purpose:** Provides a clean, neutral foundation for content

---

### Secondary Colors (Dark Mode)

#### Achievement Gold
- **Hex Code:** `#fda700`
- **Usage:** Success indicators, CTAs (Call-to-Action buttons), achievement highlights
- **Purpose:** Consistent success indicator across both light and dark modes

#### Success Forest
- **Hex Code:** `#4a6741`
- **Usage:** Growth, achievement states
- **Purpose:** Represents growth and achievement in dark mode contexts

#### Creative Depth
- **Hex Code:** `#6b4e71`
- **Usage:** Innovation, strategic thinking, creative intelligence
- **Purpose:** Conveys creativity and innovation in dark mode

#### Executive Depth
- **Hex Code:** `#1b3448`
- **Usage:** Dark mode backgrounds, premium depth
- **Purpose:** Provides sophisticated, premium background for dark mode interface

---

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Light Mode Palette
- **Primary Background:** Pure Foundation (`#f6f7f9`)
- **Primary Text/Headers:** Authority Navy (`#02295b`)
- **Accent/CTAs:** Achievement Gold (`#fda700`)
- **Growth/Creative Elements:** Strategic Forest (`#2c5f41`)

### Dark Mode Palette
- **Primary Background:** Executive Depth (`#1b3448`)
- **Accent/CTAs:** Achievement Gold (`#fda700`)
- **Success/Growth States:** Success Forest (`#4a6741`)
- **Creative/Innovation Elements:** Creative Depth (`#6b4e71`)
